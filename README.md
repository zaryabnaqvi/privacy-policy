## 5. Account Deletion

### For Customers

You can delete your account at any time through the app's Profile settings. When you delete your account:

- All active orders must be completed first
- Your Stripe customer account will be deleted
- Order history will be anonymized (records kept without personal info)
- Pending order payments will be deleted
- Your user account will be permanently deleted

### For Drivers

You can delete your account through the app's Profile settings. Requirements:

- No active deliveries in progress
- Wallet balance must be $0 (withdraw all funds first)

When you delete your driver account:

- Your Stripe Connect account will be deleted
- Driver reference will be removed from orders
- Earnings records will be anonymized (financial records kept)
- Wallet transactions will be anonymized
- Your driver wallet and account will be permanently deleted

If you cannot meet the requirements for immediate deletion, you can request account deletion for admin review.
